Margin

Use m and add "-amount", available in range 1-5.
Ex m-1 m-3
Use m and add "direction" for spacify direction , available t = top ,r = right ,b = bottom ,l = left .
Ex ml-1 mb-5
And nm for remove margin.

m-2
m-3
m-4
m-5

    <div class="mt-3"></div >

Padding

Modal

Modal is available 3 size modal-sm, modal, modal-lg.
Modal has 2 animation fade and slide.
Modal must write in structure below for use.

Trigger

    <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary btn-lg" data-toggle="modal" data-target="#modelId">
        Launch
    </button>

Body

    <div class="modal slide" id="modelId" tabindex="-1" role="dialog" aria-labelledby="modelTitleId" aria-hidden="true">
        <div class="modal-dialog" role="document">
            <div class="modal-content">
                <div class="modal-header">
                    <div class="modal-title">Modal title</div>
                        <button type="button" class="close" data-dismiss="modal" aria-label="Close">
                            <span aria-hidden="true">×</span>
                        </button>
                </div>
                <div class="modal-body">
                    Body
                </div>
                <div class="modal-footer">
                    <button type="button" class="btn btn-secondary" data-dismiss="modal">Close</button>
                    <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary">Save</button>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
